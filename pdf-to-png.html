Frequently Asked Questions

Why convert PDF to PNG?

PNG images are universally compatible — you can paste them into presentations, share on social media, embed in documents, or use them anywhere images are accepted. PDFs aren't always accepted.

Is the quality good?

Yes. The converter renders each PDF page at 2x resolution for sharp output. Text and graphics stay crisp.

Can I convert a multi-page PDF?

Yes — every page is extracted as a separate PNG image. You can download them individually or all at once as a ZIP file.

Is it really free?

Yes, completely free with unlimited conversions. No account needed, no limits. It's open source!

Does it work with password-protected PDFs?

Currently only unprotected PDFs are supported. Remove the password protection first using your PDF viewer.
