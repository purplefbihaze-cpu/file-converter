Why convert HEIC to JPG?

What is HEIC?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's default photo format on iPhones since iOS 11. It saves storage space but isn't compatible with many apps, websites, and non-Apple devices.

Why is HEIC not compatible everywhere?

Most Windows apps, older Android phones, and many websites only accept JPG or PNG. HEIC is an Apple-specific format that requires special support to open.

Is it safe to use an online HEIC converter?

Most online converters upload your photos to their servers — which is a privacy risk. This converter processes everything locally in your browser. Your photos never leave your device.

Will the quality be preserved?

Yes. The conversion maintains high quality. JPG output uses 92% quality setting, which is virtually indistinguishable from the original at normal viewing sizes.
